⚠️ Important Disclaimer

Planthology calculates travel times and estimates based on your plan data and current location estimates. However, always double-check travel times and routes with a maps app before leaving. The app makes its best guess about your starting location based on your plans, but this could be inaccurate due to GPS uncertainty, location services settings, or unexpected delays. Planthology is a planning tool to help you organize your day; it should not be your only source of travel information. Real-world conditions, traffic, and circumstances may differ significantly from estimates.

FAQ

Why don't I get "time to leave" notifications?

Three things need to be true: the plan has a location, notifications are allowed for Planthology in iOS Settings, and "Leave-by notifications" is on in the app's Settings → Notifications. Setting a home base helps too; it's where your first drive of the day starts from.

What's the Live Activity countdown, and why is it off?

It's an optional Lock Screen / Dynamic Island countdown that appears as your leave-by time approaches. It's off by default to keep things quiet; turn it on in Settings → Notifications.

What's the difference between a plan and a reminder?

A plan has a time and usually a place; it participates in drive-time scheduling. A reminder is just "ping me at this time" for things without an address, with Done / Snooze / Ignore right on the notification.

Is my shared trip really private?

Yes. Trips are end-to-end encrypted before upload; the key is only in the invite link you share. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I get the trail search working?

Trail search uses the free National Park Service API. Get a key at nps.gov/subjects/developer (takes about a minute) and paste it in Settings → NPS Trail Search API Key.

How do I free up storage?

Settings → Clean Up History deletes past everyday plans and their photos by month, or everything before today. Trips are never touched.
