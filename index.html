Planthology is one planner for everything on your calendar — the workweek, the weekend errands, and the big trip — with drive-time smarts that tell you exactly when to head out the door.

iOS 26.4+ · iPhone · free of ads and tracking

Everything in one agenda

Most planners handle your appointments or your vacations. Planthology does both — and it understands the drive between them.

🕒

Leave-by intelligence

Set a home base and every plan with a location gets a live drive-time estimate. A notification tells you when to leave — with Start directions, Snooze, and Ignore right on it. Optional Live Activity puts a countdown on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

📆

Your week at a glance

A seven-day agenda with recurring events ("gym every weekday at 6"), overlap warnings, buffers for things you can't be late to, and a live progress bar through today.

🔔

Reminders

Not everything has an address. Reminders ping you at the right moment — clean the house, book the hotel — and you mark them Done, Snooze, or Ignore straight from the notification.

🗺️

Trips, day by day

Build itineraries with lodging, flights, hikes, and food. Every day gets a map and drive legs between stops. Import GPX routes or search National Park Service trails to fill in hike stats automatically.

🔐

Private sharing

Share a trip with your travel companions — view-only or collaborative. Trip data is end-to-end encrypted; the server never sees your plans.

✨

Reviews worth posting

Month in Review and Trip Review turn your plans and photos into a shareable card — stats, a pin map of everywhere you went, and your favorite shots — sized for Instagram, Facebook, or Messages.

About the developer

Hi, I'm Mitchell Isler — an software engineer who builds apps to solve his own problems first and polishes them until they're worth sharing. Planthology started as a vacation itinerary tool and grew into the planner I use for everything, every day.

I care about software that respects people: local-first data, end-to-end encryption where your information leaves the device, no ads, no tracking.

Want to work together or see more? Find me on GitHub, or open an issue to get in touch.
