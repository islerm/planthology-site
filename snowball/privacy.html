Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Snowball Budget is as private as an app can be: it has no accounts, no servers, and makes no network connections at all. There are no ads, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

What stays on your device

Everything: your income sources, fixed costs, category budgets, expenses, savings goals and contributions, loans, and payoff plan are stored in the app's local database on your iPhone. That data is included in your normal device backups (iCloud or computer backups, per your iOS settings) and is never sent anywhere by the app.

No bank connections

Snowball Budget does not link to bank accounts, credit cards, or any financial institution. You enter your numbers yourself, which means no third party ever handles your credentials or transactions on the app's behalf.

Your choices

You can edit or delete any entry at any time. Deleting the app deletes all of its local data.

Children

Snowball Budget is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new date above.
