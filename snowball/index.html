Budgeting without the spreadsheet

Five tabs, one loop: plan the month, log what you spend, and watch one number — Left to Spend.

🧮

Plan your month

Start in the Planner: income minus fixed costs, loan payments, and your savings goal leaves what's yours to allocate. Split that across category budgets and the month is set.

🧭

Budget your way

Pick the style the app coaches you toward: zero-based (every dollar has a job), 50/30/20 (needs / wants / saving), kakeibo (save first, reflect monthly), strict envelopes (unspent money rolls over), or flexible tracking with no rules. Switching is always safe — only the guidance changes, never your data.

🧾

Log expenses in two taps

Add an expense, give it a category, done. The more you log, the smarter every chart gets — spending by category, recent activity, and how the month is tracking.

❄️

Snowball your debt

Enter your loans, pick snowball (smallest balance first — quick wins) or avalanche (highest rate first — least interest), and commit an extra monthly amount. The payoff plan compares both strategies side by side, shows the interest each saves, which loan your extra dollars target right now, and the month you'll be debt-free.

🏦

Watch your jar fill

Set a monthly savings goal and log contributions as you go. Your jar fills up right on the Dashboard — a small win you can see every day.

📊

One number to watch

The Dashboard shows Left to Spend — your spending budget minus what you've logged. Fixed costs don't count against it; your plan already set them aside. When it's green, you're fine. That's the whole day-to-day.

📅

The annual picture

When you're curious about the big picture, the Annual tab has your whole year — income, spending, and savings, month by month.
